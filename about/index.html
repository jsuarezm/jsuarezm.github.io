About me

Hello, welcome to my blog! My name is Jesús Alberto Suárez, I am an information security professional with strong preference by open source solutions and nowadays by DevOps.

I am also interested on risk assessment and management, vulnerability management, information security, security technology, ethical hacking and deployment techniques.

As risk evaluator I have learned to build a profile of a company, so themes as business model and process management are also of my concern. I like all about continuous improvement, in special how to use Agile, Lean and kanban on IT or Security, in combination with ITIL or ISO27001.

Regarding DevOps, I like all the tools used to implement the Continuous integration or delivery, as GIT, Puppet, Chef, Ansible, Jenkins, and all the environments based on virtualization or cloud. I don´t like the term SecOps, I prefer to use Continuous Security as a set of activities to be performed along the development and the operation.
